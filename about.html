About Us

Downballot Solutions was founded with one central goal: reduce barriers to entry for individuals seeking local office by providing high quality campaign resources at an affordable rate. We've worked with dozens of local campaigns in seven states.

Too often, aspiring civil servants are deterred from campaigning by exorbitant costs or an unclear path to victory. We empower local activists and neighborhood organizers to make a difference in their municipalities -- providing resources usually only afforded to campaigns for higher office.

Local Matters

Political decisions made at the local level directly and immediately affect our lives.

Sadly, the deterioration of local politics in America is well documented. With outsized attention on the national gridlock and a sharp decline in local journalism, truly grassroots efforts lack the resources and media attention they once had.

Downballot Solutions is singularly focused on solving local problems. We are not beholden to a national entity and only support local candidates and campaigns.

Data is for Everyone

Voter data rolls can be purchased from state-level Secretary of State offices but are unwieldy and difficult to use. Alternatively, candidates can turn to party apparatuses for expensive voter analyses.

We democratize voter data. Every Downballot client is afforded full access to a digestible and aesthetically pleasing view of their municipality’s voter data.

Nonpartisanship

Most school board and city council ballots do not include party labels for a reason. When local policymakers are beholden or indebted to national party apparatuses, they represent Washington’s interests more than their community’s.

Unfortunately, party tools are too often the only high-quality options available to local campaigns.

We empower local activists seeking nonpartisan office by giving them unequivocally nonpartisan support.
